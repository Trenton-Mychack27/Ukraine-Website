Pair Grounding

Target Age Group: 5â€“12 years old

Objective: To help children feeling panic and stress to ground themselves while also feeling the support of a peer.

Why it Works

  • Grounding: Tangible focus that distracts from anxiety
  • Symbolic Connection: Links children to others and self
  • Mindfulness-Based: Empowers self-regulation
  • Universal & Simple: Can be used anywhere

Instructions:

  • Share 5 things they can hear, smell, touch, and see.
  • Form pairs or small groups.
  • Create a safe space with respectful atmosphere.
  • Play music to reduce stress.
  • Use mindfulness techniques to focus and calm.
  • Follow-up impact: Children can use this any time, anywhere.
