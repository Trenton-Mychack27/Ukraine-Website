About Us

We are a team of computer science and psychology students from Siena College located in Loudonville, New York. We are passionate about creating tools that make a real difference. This website is the result of our collaboration with Psychology students, as well as pre-service teachers from Vinnitsa State pedagogical. We’re honored to support Ukrainian pre-service teachers as they prepare to make a difference in the classroom. This project is our way of giving back — combining design, techniques, and purpose to create something meaningful for future educators. We believe in the power of teachers and the impact they have on communities, and it’s a privilege to contribute, even in a small way, to their journey.
